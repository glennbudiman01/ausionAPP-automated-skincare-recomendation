Services

AUSION is a beauty website powered by AI technology (Artificial Intelligence) which can recognize problems on your face, a website can help provide skincare recommendations based on facial problems detected, and it is supported by image data that has more than 2000 data as a reference for the system to make decisions. This website also includes a chatbot feature that allows users to easily obtain general information about the website.

What make us different from others?

The output of our system offers skincare products from various categories of skincare products, ranging from serum, moisturizer, and toner, to cleanser, so that the solution given can be maximized.

What to do

Go through our try-on web apps. You access it by clicking on the “Try Now” button.

Input your current skin condition detail.

Prepare and upload your front side photo. Keep your face without make up and should be in good light.

Wait and see our recommendation about skin products for you.
